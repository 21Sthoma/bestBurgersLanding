About Us

Since 1990 Best Burgers has served the Lakewood/Tacoma area with quality food and exceptional customer service. From the very beginning we have been a family owned and operated business with the goal of offering the best food at competitive prices. We use only 100% ground beef patties, flame- broiled for that down home flavor. Though burgers are our specialty we have a diverse menu, including chicken, fish and seafood. We use local products from locally based companies and we are committed to environmentally safe procedures throughout our restaurant.
